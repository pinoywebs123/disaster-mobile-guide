About Us

    The Disaster Preparedness App is a proposed thesis of the students taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Asia Technological School of Science and Arts. This application software focuses on disaster awareness and management.


The Objectives are as follows:

1.) Provide handy electronic resources to the public that can be applied in times of emergencies.

2.) Increase the public awareness about disasters.

3.) Use key learning points for preparedness activities such as knowledge for every individual before, during and after disasters.
